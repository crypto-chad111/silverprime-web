# PART 1 — THE STORY

## The Elevator Pitch

> *"You already carry the most powerful AI in the world in your pocket. We built it a body."*

The Silver Prime AIPC isn't a drone. It's a presence.

It's the difference between your AI assistant hearing you through a phone speaker — and your AI assistant being in the room with you, watching, listening, reacting, and moving through the world by your side.

---

## The Problem

Every AI assistant today has the same fundamental flaw.

It's stuck.

Siri lives in a rectangle. Alexa is bolted to a shelf. ChatGPT is a browser tab. They are powerful, brilliant — and completely blind to the physical world unless you hold a camera up to it, type a description, or tell them exactly what they're looking at.

They can't follow you. They can't see what you see. They can't go where you go.

Your phone gets closest — it's always with you, always on, always learning. That's why we built Silver Prime as a phone-first AI. Always listening. Always learning. Always in your pocket.

But even your phone has one limitation: it can only see what you point it at.

**What if your AI could see everything?**

---

## The Insight

The answer wasn't to build another AI model. We already have one — Phi-3-mini running entirely on-device, no cloud, no data leaks, no subscriptions.

The answer was to give that AI a body.

Not a humanoid robot. Not a clunky quadcopter with a GoPro strapped to it.

A ring. A floating ring that lifts silently from your shoulder, orbits your world, and sends everything it sees directly to the Silver Prime AI running on your phone.

Your phone IS the brain. The AIPC is the eyes, the ears, and the presence.

---

## The Product

**AIPC** — *AI Personal Companion*

A ring-form drone unlike anything that exists. Approximately 130mm across. Under 250 grams — meaning no FAA registration required in the US, no drone licence in most countries.

It does something no other consumer drone does:

It lives with you.

Not parked on a shelf waiting for a weekend flight. Not charging in a case in your car. On your shoulder — on the magnetic charging platform — ready to lift on command, scout ahead, watch your back, scan your environment, and return home when you're done.

**"Hey Silver, eyes up."**

Three words. The AIPC lifts from your shoulder, rises to eye level, and starts streaming 1080p HD video to your Silver Prime app. The on-device AI processes what it sees in real time. No cloud. No lag. No privacy compromise.

---

## How It Works

```
You speak → Silver Prime app (on your phone) processes the command
         → routes to AIPC via Wi-Fi Direct (no router needed)
         → AIPC executes: lift, scan, follow, hold position, return
         → HD video streams back to your phone in real time
         → AI analyses the feed, narrates, flags, responds
```

**The ring-form design isn't just aesthetic** — it's functional. The four rotor modules are embedded inside the ring wall, protected by the frame itself. The hollow centre reduces drag and creates a distinctive visual signature. The camera is flush-mounted at the equator — forward-facing, always level, always stable.

**The Shoulder Platform** is where the magic lives between flights. A slim carbon fibre cross-body harness with a magnetic charging pad on the shoulder. The AIPC lands on the pad automatically when called home. Wireless charging begins the moment it touches down. USB-C backup for fast top-up. The drone is always charged, always ready, always exactly where you left it.

---

## The Technology

**On-device AI (your phone):**
- Phi-3-mini-4k Q4_K_M — a 2.2GB language model running entirely on your Android device
- Zero cloud dependency. Zero data sent externally. Your conversations stay on your device.
- Cloud fallback via Groq (Llama 3.1, Mixtral) for complex queries when you choose it

**The AIPC drone:**
- Linux SBC for flight control — lightweight, reliable, open
- "Hey Silver" wakeword — always listening via Porcupine by Picovoice
- Wi-Fi Direct connection to your phone — no router, no internet required
- HD 1080p camera, RTSP stream, sub-200ms latency
- GPS for outdoor precision, altitude hold for indoor stability
- 10–15 minute flight time per charge
- Under 250g — below FAA registration threshold in the US

**The result:** A pocket AI that can see.

## The Vision

We are not building a drone company.

We are building the first AI that genuinely lives with you.

The AIPC is version one. The physical interface for an AI that will grow with you, learn your routines, anticipate your needs, and over time become the most useful thing you own — not because it's clever in a chat window, but because it is *present* in your world.

In two years, it will recognise faces. In three, it will pre-emptively launch when it detects a situation worth flagging. In five, the v2 hardware will be smaller, quieter, smarter — and the Shoulder Platform will be a fashion statement, not an accessory.

We're starting with a drone. We're building a companion.

## SECTION 15: THE RISKS

We're going to be honest with you. Here's what could go wrong and what we're doing about it.

**Manufacturing complexity**
Making 1,000 ring-form drones in a new factory is genuinely difficult. We've built 15 pre-production units. We know where the hard parts are. We've budgeted 20% contingency into every production estimate.

**Component supply**
Electronics supply chains can be unpredictable. We're identifying two qualified suppliers for every critical component before we lock the design.

**Flight time**
Current prototypes achieve 10–12 minutes. Our target is 15 minutes. Battery chemistry is improving. We'll ship what we have and improve it in v1.1 — we won't delay the campaign waiting for perfect.

**Regulatory**
The AIPC weighs under 250g with no payload, which keeps it in the most permissive regulatory category in most countries. We're designing to EASA Class C0 standards — the strictest relevant framework — so we're ready for every major market.

**Delays**
If we hit a delay, you'll hear about it from us before it happens — not after. Monthly backer updates. No surprises.

## SECTION 16: FAQ

**Q: Does the AIPC require an internet connection?**
No. It connects to your phone via Wi-Fi Direct — no router, no internet. The on-device AI runs entirely on your Android device. You can use it in the middle of nowhere.

**Q: What Android phones are compatible?**
Any Android phone capable of running Phi-3-mini (we recommend 8GB RAM minimum). A compatibility list will be published before the campaign closes.

**Q: Do I need a drone licence?**
In the US: No. Under 250g is below the FAA registration threshold. In most EU countries: No. EASA Class C0 exemption applies to drones under 250g. Always check your local regulations.

**Q: Is my data private?**
The on-device AI (Phi-3-mini) processes everything locally. Nothing is sent to any server. If you use the Groq cloud fallback, only the specific query you ask is sent — not video, not history, not your personal data.

**Q: When does it ship?**
Pioneer Pack backers: 14 months after campaign close. All backers: 18 months. We know that's a wait. We'd rather underpromise and overdeliver.

**Q: Can I get a refund if there's a delay?**
Kickstarter's policy applies. We will offer a full refund if we miss our delivery window by more than 6 months, no questions asked.

**Q: What about iOS?**
AIPC works with Android. iOS is not currently planned — Silver Prime is an Android-first product.

**Q: Is the Shoulder Platform required?**
No — the AIPC can take off from any flat surface. The Shoulder Platform is the premium experience, not a requirement.

**Q: What happens to my backing if the campaign doesn't hit its goal?**
Kickstarter is all-or-nothing. If we don't hit $1,500,000, no one is charged. Your card is only charged if the campaign succeeds.
