## Executive Summary

Silver Prime is launching the **AIPC Companion Drone** — a ring/toroid-form AI-powered personal drone that pairs with the Silver Prime Android AI assistant. The phone IS the brain. The drone is the eyes, ears, and physical presence.

**The mission:** Build a world-class manufacturing operation in Saudi Arabia, funded entirely by a Kickstarter campaign targeting $2.5M–$5M. No venture capital. No angels. No grants. Built by the community, for the community.

**HQ:** Saudi Arabia — King Abdullah Economic City (KAEC) or NEOM Oxagon.
**Why Saudi:** Vision 2030 industrial infrastructure, 0% personal income tax, cheapest industrial real estate of any major economy, massive regional market, government-built industrial cities designed for exactly this kind of company.

**Kickstarter campaign target:**
- Minimum fundable goal: **$1,500,000**
- Moderate target: **$2,500,000**
- Full campaign potential (all tiers filled): **~$3,100,000**
- Super-stretch (viral coverage): **$5,000,000+**

### 1.4 Saudi Drone Regulations (GACA)

Saudi Arabia's General Authority of Civil Aviation (GACA) governs drones — separately from European EASA rules.

| Aspect | Status |
|---|---|
| Registration (<250g with camera) | Required — online, ~1 week |
| Commercial operation permit | Required — ~4–8 weeks |
| Airworthiness certificate | Not required for small drones |
| Remote ID | Requirement under development — not yet fully enforced |
| Manufacturing licence | Not required |
| Radio compliance | Must meet CITC (Saudi telecom regulator) standards |

**Design strategy:** Build the AIPC to EASA Class C0 standards as the baseline. This covers Saudi GACA requirements and simultaneously qualifies the drone for EU and UK markets. One compliance effort opens three major markets.

1. **Build the pre-launch email list hard** — target 15,000–25,000 subscribers before Day 1
